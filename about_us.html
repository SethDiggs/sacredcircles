Our
Mission &
Values

We support the sacred circles of conscious communities. When we empower the educators of our communities the necessary resources to teach in the fullness of their heart-space, our young people and families directly benefit by receiving the lessons and support which will most benefit their lives.

We're focused on providing conscious lessons, experiences and gatherings for parents, teachers and young people based on the model of universal wisdom nature provides mixed with fundamental life skills necessary to thrive in our modern society.

Ethics, integrity, transparency, accountability and honesty are some of the foundational pillars we're guided by.

Sacred Circles was founded in January 2024 for the community, by the community in South Florida. We plan to expand our community outreach in the years to come, creating a ripple effect of love worldwide.
